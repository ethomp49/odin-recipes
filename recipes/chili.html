Bean and Corn Chili

Description

An easy and delicious chili dinner made with mostly canned ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 2 lb ground beef
  • 2 oz can tomato paste
  • 32 oz can whole peeled tomatoes
  • 8 oz can black beans
  • 8 oz can kidney beans
  • 8 oz can whole kernel corn
  • 8 oz can beef broth

Spice blend:

  • 3 Tbsp chili powder
  • 2 Tbsp paprika
  • 1 Tbsp cumin
  • 1 Tbsp oregano
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:

  1. Heat a large pot with 1 Tbsp olive oil over medium-high heat
  2. Dice the onion and mince the garlic and add to pot, cooking for 2-3 min or until soft
  3. Chop the bell peppers and add to pot, cooking for 4 min
  4. Add the ground beef and cook, breaking up meat into smaller chunks, until browned and cooked through (~5min)
  5. Add the tomato paste and mix until combined, cooking for 2 min
  6. Crush the whole peeled tomatoes in a large bowl then add to the pot
  7. Add the beans, corn, and beef broth and turn the heat up to high
  8. Add the spice blend and mix until combined
  9. Once the chili is boiling, turn heat down and simmer for 30 min
  10. Serv and enjoy
