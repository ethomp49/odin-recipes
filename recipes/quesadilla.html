Chipotle Chicken Quesadilla

Description

Easy quesadillas to wow your friends in under 30 minutes! These quesadillas feature chipotle spiced chicken, scallions, and jalapenos nestled in melted white cheddar. Serve with some lime crema to really add that extra flair!

Ingredients:

Quesadilla

  • 4 fajita tortillas
  • 1 lb raw chicken breast
  • 1 bunch scallions
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1/2 lime
  • 8 oz sharp white cheddar

Seasoning Blend

  • 1 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 Tbsp paprika
  • 1/2 Tbsp cumin
  • 1/2 Tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

Lime Crema

  • 4 Tbsp sour cream
  • 1/2 lime

Directions:

  1. Unpackage the raw chicken and pat down with a paper towel until dry. Cut into smaller 1/2 in pieces.
  2. Mix chicken with seasoning blend in a medium bowl until the chicken is thoroughly coated.
  3. Add a tablespoon of olive oil to a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Once the pan is heated, add the seasoned chicken and cook, stirring occasionally and breaking up larger pieces, for 5 minutes or chicken is cooked through.
  5. While chicken cooks, wash and dry lime, scallions, and jalapeno.
  6. Trim the roots of the scallion and then thinly slice, separating whites from greens.
  7. Cut the lime into quarters.
  8. Trim the stem of the jalapeno and remove the core. Cut the jalapeno in half and remove ribs and seeds, then thinly dice.
  9. When chicken is done cooking, add to a small bowl with scallion whites and diced jalapeno.
  10. Squeeze the juice of one lime quarter over the chicken mixture and add salt and pepper to taste. Mix until combined.
  11. Wipe out pan used for chicken with a paper towel, then add another tablespoon of olive oil and return to medium-high heat.
  12. Shred the cheddar cheese and distribute evenly among one side of each of the 4 tortillas.
  13. Divide the chicken mixture evenly among the 4 tortillas as well, using the same side as the cheddar cheese.
  14. Fold the tortilla over and press firmly to close each quesadilla.
  15. Working in batches of two, add folded quesadillas to pan and cook, flipping, until each side is golden-brown.
  16. In a small bowl, combine sour cream with the juice of two lime-quarters and mix, adding salt and pepper to taste.
  17. Serve the tortillas with the lime crema using scallion greens to garnish.
