Chipotle Chicken Quesadilla

Description

Easy quesadillas to wow your friends in under 30 minutes! These quesadillas feature chipotle spiced chicken, scallions, and jalapenos nestled in melted white cheddar. Serve with some lime crema to really add that extra flair!

Ingredients

Quesadilla

  • 4 fajita tortillas
  • 1 lb raw chicken breast
  • 1 bunch scallions
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1/2 lime
  • 8 oz sharp white cheddar

Seasoning Blend

  • 1 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 Tbsp paprika
  • 1/2 Tbsp cumin
  • 1/2 Tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

Lime Crema

  • 4 Tbsp sour cream
  • 1/2 lime
